Our Story

A Legacy of Inspection Excellence

IQS S.r.l. was established in 2015 to give continuity to decades of inspection and expediting expertise. Before founding IQS, the management team delivered services to Snamprogetti, Saipem, ENI, Esso, Statoil and others since 1990.

In July 2016, the shareholders acquired Projeco Italia S.r.l. (Rome, est. 2006). Full merger by incorporation completed September 2018.

Today, IQS operates with approximately 600 inspectors and engineers managed from our TOCC Centre in San Donato Milanese, Italy.
